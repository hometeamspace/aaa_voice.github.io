About us

AAA Voice and Data are your one stop communications and IT consultants. Our staff have over 10 years experience and bring a wealth of knowledge to provide best possible outcomes for our customers. As consultants we are not bound to one service provider thus providing options from the best in traditional telephony and internet providers through to the best in VOIP services.

Along with service provider options AAA Voice and Data also offer a range of telephone systems, Cable Services, Surveillance Camera Solutions and IT infrastructure from micro business solutions through to enterprise solutions.

Phone Systems

Ericsson-LG’s iPECS unified Communication Solution (UCS) is a powerful multimedia collaboration and productivity enhancing tool designed for the small to large enterprise. It’s single server architecture helps SMB’s start experiencing the next generation communication solution without investing in enterprise level equipment. The rich features, capability and the ability integrate 3rd party solutions are well suited for large enterprises’ UC environment.

Cabling Services

Do you need new data cabling installed quickly and efficiently? The team from AAA Voice and Data are fully licensed and experienced in all cabling requirements for both small homes to large offices and government buildings. Adding new network cabling/data/phone points gives you the benefits of a wired network that allows for greater speed for file sharing, streaming media, gaming and security camera monitoring. From the maintenance and repair of computer cabling and phone systems to a full network cabling installation. Our team have a dedicated cable expert who can get the job done right.

SO Wifi
